Why would I need the interface of everhelper.me at all if all of its
features are available in the add-ons?

Of course, you can enjoy the functionality of everhelper.me without
accessing the Web interface though it does have several specific
features that may be of great use to you. For instance, it allows:

  • Creating additional backups
  • Creating private bookmarks and dials
  • Importing bookmarks from HTML files
  • Copying bookmarks into and from FVD Speed Dial

Plus, it gives you the access to your bookmarks from absolutely any
browser, even one without our apps installed!
